AI in Journalism: The Ethics and Usages of Artificial Intelligence for Facts and Statistics within Journalism

March 2024 · White Paper · 15 min read

While AI offers significant benefits for data analysis and content generation in journalism, it introduces complex ethical challenges through algorithmic bias and misinformation. This white paper examines how AI bias manifests in writing, interpretation, and personalization within newsrooms, and proposes a multi-faceted approach including algorithmic accountability, bias detection systems, and collaboration with tech companies to maintain journalistic integrity and public trust.
